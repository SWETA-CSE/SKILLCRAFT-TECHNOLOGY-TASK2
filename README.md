# SKILLCRAFT-TECHNOLOGY-TASK2
Task 2 Completed | Logo Design

Sharing my second task from my Graphic Design Internship at SkillCraft Technology.

For this task, I developed a logo and basic brand identity for Homefolk — a home décor and lifestyle concept built around sustainable, handmade, and mindful living.

The idea was to create something that feels warm, simple, and connected to home. I used a house outline to represent comfort and belonging, while the leaf element reflects nature, sustainability, and growth.

For the overall visual direction, I chose earthy greens, warm brown, and soft cream tones to give the brand a calm and natural feel. The handwritten-style typography was selected to make the identity feel more personal and welcoming.

This project helped me understand how every design choice—from the icon and font to the colour palette—works together to communicate a brand’s story.

Tools used: Canva and Freepik
